How to add real photos

Create an images folder next to this file, drop in your photos, then set each aircraft's image value below to something like images/citation-x.jpg. Use your own photos or properly licensed images if you plan to share it publicly.

Tip: start with side profiles. Corporate jets get much easier when you train yourself to notice engine count/location, T-tail shape, winglets, cabin window pattern, nose length, and whether the fuselage looks “tube-like,” “fat,” or “long and sleek.”
